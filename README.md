## What it is

REDLINE is contract review and redlining for law firms and in-house legal teams that cannot put privileged documents into a third-party cloud LLM. The whole pipeline runs on the firm's own hardware. No cloud API. No third-party inference. No documents leaving the network. Mathematical data sovereignty: the data never even hits the internet.

Built for the firms that audited cloud-AI contract review tools, found they violate privilege protocols, and walked away.
